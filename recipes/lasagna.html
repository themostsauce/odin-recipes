Lasagna

Description

The best and only lasagna recipe you'll ever need! This recipe is everything you want and love in a lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Veggies

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente
  2. Layer the pasta in a baking dish with cheese and sauce, repeating until you've reached the top and springkly with cheese
  3. Bake until the top is golden brown and serve with fresh basil
